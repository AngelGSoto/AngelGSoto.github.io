About Me

I am Luis Angel Gutiérrez Soto, an astronomer with extensive experience in observational astrophysics. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Instituto de Astrofísica de La Plata, CONICET -- UNLP, in La Plata, Argentina, under the supervision of Favio R. Faifer and Claudia Lucia Mendes de Oliveira.

Previously, I held a postdoctoral position at the Instituto de Astronomia, Geofísica e Ciências Atmosféricas (IAG), Universidade de São Paulo, Brazil, where I worked with Claudia Lucia Mendes de Oliveira.

I completed my Ph.D. in Astronomy at Observatório do Valongo, Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, under the guidance of Denise Rocha Gonçalves and Stavros Akras. My master's degree in Astrophysics was obtained from Centro de Radioastronomía y Astrofísica (CRYA), Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM), Morelia, Michoacán, Mexico, under the supervision of William John Henney.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Education in Mathematics and Physics from Universidad Popular del Cesar (UPC), Valledupar, Cesar, Colombia, where I was advised by Elio Fabio Mejía Henao. I also obtained a degree as a Superior Normalist with Emphasis in Spanish Language from Escuela Normal Superior María Inmaculada, Manaure, Cesar, Colombia.
